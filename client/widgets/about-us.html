Mission Statement

Conversa strives to provide a safe and friendly environment where users can chat without the concern of being identified.


About Us

Conversa is a firm based in Boston, Massachusetts. Founded in February 2018 by three Wentworth Institute of Technology students, Conversa specializes in anonymous messaging. With Conversa, you can chat with other users about a wide range of topics with the comfort of doing so anonymously. Whether you wish to disclose a sensitive matter you are going through, meet new people, or simply want have a conversation with someone but are hesitant to do so face-to-face, look no further than Conversa.


Additional Information

Conversa is a Portuguese word meaning "conversation".
